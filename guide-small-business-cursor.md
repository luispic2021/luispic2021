## Step 1: Why Cursor for your business

Other AI tools are like a smart friend you have to copy-paste with: you describe something, they answer, and you have to manually put that answer into a doc or spreadsheet. Cursor is different: **you work in a folder of your own files, and the AI can read and edit those files.** So when you say "turn my notes into a one-pager," it can see your notes, create or update a file, and the result is already where you need it — no copy-paste.

Think of it as:
- **A folder** = your project (e.g. "My Business")
- **Files inside** = your ideas, one-pagers, checklists, drafts
- **The chat** = where you ask the AI to help. It can read your files and create or change them.

You're not coding. You're writing and organizing; the AI is your partner that can draft, refine, and keep everything in one place.

---

## Step 2: Get set up

1. **Download Cursor** — Go to [cursor.com/download](https://cursor.com/download) and install the desktop app (not the web version).
2. **Open a new project** — In Cursor: File → New Window (or "Open project"), then "New Folder." Name it something like "My Business" and click Open.
3. **Start a conversation** — You'll see a chat area (often on the side or bottom). Click "New agent" or start typing in the chat box. Make sure you're in **Agent** mode (there's a dropdown near the chat box) so the AI can create and edit files for you.
4. **Optional: introduce yourself** — In your first message, you can say: "I'm starting a small business. I'm not a coder — I just want to use you to draft documents, checklists, and ideas. Only create or edit simple text/markdown files, no code." That sets the tone for every conversation in this project.

## Step 3: Your first Cursor prompt

Your first real task: turn one sentence about your business into a short paragraph you could use on a website or in a pitch.

**What to do:**
1. In the chat, paste this (and replace the example with your own one-sentence idea):

```
I'm starting a small business. In one sentence, my idea is: [e.g. "I sell handmade candles at local markets and online."]

Please write a short paragraph (3–4 sentences) that describes what I do, who it's for, and why it matters. Write it in a warm, clear tone. Save it in a file called "my-business-in-one-paragraph.md" in this project.
```

2. Send the message. The AI will create the file. Open it from the file list to see your paragraph.
3. If you want changes, just say in the chat: "Make it shorter" or "Emphasize the local/eco angle" — and the AI can edit the same file.

**Why this matters:** You've just used Cursor the way you'll use it for everything else: you asked in plain English, and the result landed in a file in your project. No coding, no copying — just ask and refine.
